Breakdown

Craving Crustacean

  • Lead rigger, animator, visual developer
  • Character designed and animated to look powerless and pathetic with his tiny hind shell, implying his sadness at the beginning
  • Small shell allowed him to emote like a dog, so he was animated as very flighty and excited

  • Rigging Breakdown

    • Claws and legs are hard body segments with reversible IK / FK controls
    • Body is smoothly bound for smooth, exaggerated curves in spine, creating more expressive silhouettes
    • Eyes smoothly bound. Blend shapes for expressions, lattice controls for squash and stretch, and cluster controls along a curve in the eyestalk to control their arcs.
    • Shell is detachable and full body lattice for squash and stretch

Spawning Portal

  • Created in Houdini, Nuke
  • Several particle simulations in Houdini composited in Nuke. Hex Grid was projected onto a rounded surface to bulge outward, with its visibility controlled by whether it was inside an expanding and contracting sphere.

Volumetric Ray Tracer - Wisp

  • Written in C++
  • Custom volumetric ray tracer that uses standard techniques to calculate light extinction in a volume. Wisp created by a technique that stamps density into a voxel gridby applying several layers of simplex noise to randomly distributed density dots along a unit sphere.

Galaxy Simulation

  • Art directable simulation of the n-body problem run with OpenGL and accelerated in OpenCL.
  • 9216 total particles.
  • Gravity force is calculated for each particle based on the mass and distance of other particles.
  • Art direction favored over physical accuracy, so a vector field was added to nudges particles into the form of spiral galaxies most people are familiar with.
  • Parameter input files allow for different simulations by altering features like the particle's initial position, the gravitational constant, and the strength of the vector field.

Procedural Hex Grid Topology

  • Created in Houdini
  • Hex grid extruded with layered noise
  • Height of extrusion fed into shader to alter attributes

Rocket FX

  • Created in Houdini

  • Explosion created in a second simulation
